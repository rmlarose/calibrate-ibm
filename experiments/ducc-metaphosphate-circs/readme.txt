I used Exachem to generate DUCC effective hamiltonians where I freezed the 10 core orbitals,
included all valence occupied orbitals in active space, and 8 virtual orbitals in the active
space. This provides a 56 qubit problem. I used the 6-31G* basis set for the downfolding.

The CCSD(T)/6-31G* (full Hamiltonian) energies with frozen-core approximation 
was computed with PySCF and reported in reference-energies.txt.
